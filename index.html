Frequently Asked Questions

Where is the conference being held?

The conference will be held at the Grand Tech Center in Silicon Valley. Detailed directions, parking information, and hotel recommendations will be emailed to you immediately after registration.

Are meals included in the ticket price?

Yes! All Standard and Pro tickets include daily catered networking lunches, all-day coffee, and snacks. Pro ticket holders also get exclusive access to the VIP networking dinner on the second night.

What is your refund policy?

We offer full refunds up to 30 days before the event begins. Cancellations made within 30 days of the event are non-refundable, but you can transfer your ticket to a colleague or friend at no extra cost.

Will the sessions be recorded?

Absolutely. All keynote and breakout sessions will be professionally recorded. Standard tickets include 30 days of digital access to the recordings, while Pro and Team tickets include lifetime access.
